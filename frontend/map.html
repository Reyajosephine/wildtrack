Restrooms

Available in Zones A, C, and D with clear signposts from all major walkways.

First Aid

Main first-aid station is next to Ranger HQ, with support carts on safari trails.

Shuttle Stops

Electric carts run every 15 minutes between entry gate, Zone B, and Zone D.
